Our Mission

MAINTEN-
ANCE IS THE
FOUNDATION.

At Adapt Performance Solutions, we believe that proper maintenance is the foundation of performance. Whether you're maintaining your OEM daily driver, enhancing your street-performance build, or pushing the limits with a high-performance track setup, our maintenance kits are designed to keep your vehicle running at its best.

Modern performance isn't just about modifications — it's about longevity, reliability, and precision. Generic kits pull from a catalog. Ours are built chassis-up, matched to your engine's displacement, oil capacity, thermal load, and service interval. Every fluid choice is spec'd for how that platform actually lives.

We're enthusiasts. We're builders. We're in the same garages and at the same track days. We built Adapt because we couldn't find something that did this right — so we made it ourselves.

01
Chassis-Specific by Default
Every kit is engineered for a specific platform — oil capacity, viscosity, filter fitment, and drain hardware are all matched before you even check out. No cross-referencing required.
02
Tiered for Your Build Level
Daily driver, OEM+ street build, or full track car — the three-tier system means you're not over-spending for street use or under-speccing for what your engine actually needs.
03
Top-Shelf Fluids Only
Motul, HKS, Nismo, ARP — brands the community trusts, not whoever offered the best margin. If it doesn't belong under your hood, it doesn't belong in our catalog.
04
Passion-Driven Business
We're a small, owner-operated business — not a warehouse dropshipper. Every order is packed with care and every product listing is written by someone who actually works on these cars.

Our Story

PASSION
DRIVEN.

Adapt started the way most good things do — out of frustration. We kept running into the same problem: sourcing the right fluids and hardware for our builds meant hours of cross-referencing specs, checking forums, and hoping whoever put the product listing together knew what they were talking about.

We knew the specs. We knew the platforms. So we started building kits the way we'd want them — pre-spec'd, tiered by use case, and complete from the jump. No hunting for a crush washer separately. No guessing if the filter threads match. Everything in one order, ready to go.

Adapt Performance Solutions grew out of that process. What started as kits for our own builds became a catalog serving the broader JDM, sport compact, and performance community. Husband and wife, built together, driven by the same love for this scene.

And Equipt — our branding division — came from the same instinct: if we're going to do something, do it right and make it look the part.
