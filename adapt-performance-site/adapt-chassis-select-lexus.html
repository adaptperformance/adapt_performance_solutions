Manufacturer Profile

LEXUS

Intelligent Sports · Luxury Performance · Japan

Refined luxury with track-honed precision — Lexus has built a performance legacy defined by balance, emotion, and driver connection. Each model represents a chapter in that story: machines engineered not just for power, but for the thrill of control behind the wheel.

The IS lineage brought precision and engagement to the compact sedan segment, blending nimble handling with Lexus's signature refinement. The GS carried performance into the executive class, merging smooth power delivery with chassis confidence for spirited grand touring. Meanwhile, the SC coupe offered elegant, driver-focused GT performance — a harmonious mix of style, comfort, and acceleration.

The RC and RC F take Lexus performance into the modern era. From the naturally aspirated GR V6 in the RC to the hand-built 5.0L V8 in the RC F, these cars deliver raw analog excitement. On track, the RC F GT500 dominated Super GT competition, proving Lexus can translate luxury into race-winning engineering.
