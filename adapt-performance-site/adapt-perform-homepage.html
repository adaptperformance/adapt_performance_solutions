What's Right for You

THREE TIERS.
ONE STANDARD.

Every kit is chassis-specific, spec'd with fluids and filters chosen for your platform — not just what fits.

Tier 01
ECO / OEM

Motul Eco-Lite or OEM-equivalent spec. Full reliability, sensible spend.

  • Motul ECO-lite or OEM-grade oil
  • OEM or OEM-spec filter
  • Crush washer / drain hardware
  • For daily-driven, stock builds
Browse ECO kits
Tier 02
Street Performance

Full synthetic high-performance oil with a premium filter. The enthusiast's baseline.

  • Motul 8100 or 300V full synthetic
  • HKS, NISMO, or OEM-spec filter
  • Copper or aluminum crush washer
  • Built for OEM+ and light-mod builds
Browse Street kits
Tier 03
Track / Purpose Built

Race-grade protection for boosted, high-rpm, or track-day builds that demand more.

  • Motul 300V Competition or Racing
  • High-flow filter: HKS, Nismo, ARP hardware
  • Spec'd for turbo / high-compression engines
  • For track, time attack, and drag builds
