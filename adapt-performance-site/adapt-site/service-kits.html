Understanding the Tiers

THREE TIERS.
ONE STANDARD.

Tier 01
ECO / OEM

OEM-equivalent spec. Full reliability, sensible spend for daily drivers and stock builds.

  • Motul ECO-lite or OEM-grade oil
  • OEM or OEM-spec filter
  • Crush washer / drain hardware
  • Chassis-matched fill volume
  • Daily driver and stock build focused
Starting from $89.98
Tier 02
OEM+ / Street

Full synthetic with a premium filter. The enthusiast baseline — built for OEM+ and lightly modded street cars.

  • Motul 8100 full synthetic
  • HKS, NISMO, or OEM-spec filter
  • Copper or aluminum crush washer
  • Chassis-matched fill volume
  • For OEM+ and lightly modified builds
Starting from $119.98
Tier 03
Track / Purpose Built

Race-grade protection for boosted, high-rpm, or track-day builds that demand more from every service interval.

  • Motul 300V Competition or Racing
  • High-flow filter: HKS, Nismo
  • ARP hardware option available
  • Spec'd for turbo and high-compression
  • For track, time attack, and drag builds
Starting from $189.98
